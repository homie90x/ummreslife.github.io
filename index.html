Virtual Tours of Our Halls

Can't visit in person? Take a virtual tour of each residence hall and find the community that fits you best.

🏛️
Featured Hall

Independence Hall

Indy is favored by student-athletes and those who appreciate its location close to the Cougar Sports and Regional Fitness Center, the Dining Hall, and the Campus Convenience Store.

Take Virtual Tour →
🌿

Blakely Hall

Blakely Hall is situated in between the Dining Hall and the Campus Mall. It is home to 30 upper-division residents in single rooms. In collaboration with the Morris Food Justice Initiative, each resident room in this hall includes a small refrigerator/freezer unit.

Take Virtual Tour →
⭐

Spooner Hall

Spooner Hall is conveniently located in the center of campus and close to the Campus Mall green space. It houses 90 first-year residents and floor options include gender-designated and gender-inclusive.

Take Virtual Tour →
🌲

Gay Hall

Clayton A. Gay Hall is composed of two towers that house 115 upper-division residents. All resident floors have two air-conditioned lounges which include study areas, dry-erase boards, and television. Clayton A. Gay Hall is conveniently located near the Cougar Sports and Regional Fitness Center and the Dining Hall.

Take Virtual Tour →
🏠

The Apartments

Suite-style apartment living for upper-class and graduate students looking for more independence.

Take Virtual Tour →
🏠

Green Prarie

Green Prairie Community is our newest residential life facility and houses 72 upper-division residents.
